Remembers your last-used card sort when viewing your deck. Without this mod, the game always resets to "Obtained ascending" sort when you open your deck.

It also highlights what the current sort is so it's easier to see/remember. This highlight can be disabled in the mod settings.

My Mods
- SlayTheStats: https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/349 - stats tracking
- ExitToDesktop: https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/553 - qol
- RememberCardSort: https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/801 - qol
- MoreTino: https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/753 - memes

Requirements
- BaseLib v3.1.0 or later: https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/103

Installation
1. Download and extract BaseLib into your mods folder if you haven't already: https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/103
2. Download RememberCardSort-vX.X.X.zip from the Files tab and extract it into your mods folder -- you should end up with a RememberCardSort/ folder inside mods/ containing RememberCardSort.dll, RememberCardSort.json, and RememberCardSort.pck.

The remembered sort is stored in RememberCardSort.cfg next to your STS2 save data:
- Windows: %APPDATA%\Slay the Spire 2\mod_configs\
- Linux: ~/.local/share/Slay the Spire 2/mod_configs/

Uninstalling
Delete the RememberCardSort/ folder from your mods directory. Optionally delete RememberCardSort.cfg from mod_configs/ (paths above) to wipe the remembered sort.
