# RememberCardSort

## My Mods

- [SlayTheStats](https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/349) — stats tracking
- [ExitToDesktop](https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/553) — qol
- RememberCardSort — qol *(this mod)*
- [MoreTino](https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/753) — memes

---

Remembers your last-used card sort when viewing your deck. Without this mod, the game always resets to "Obtained ascending" sort when you open your deck.

It also highlights what the current sort is so it's easier to see/remember. This highlight can be disabled in the mod settings.

## Requirements

- [BaseLib](https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/103) v3.1.0 or later.

## Installation

1. Download and extract [BaseLib](https://www.nexusmods.com/slaythespire2/mods/103) (v3.1.0 or later) into your mods folder if you haven't already.
2. Download `RememberCardSort-vX.X.X.zip` from the releases page and extract it into your STS2 `mods/` folder. The result should be a `RememberCardSort/` folder inside `mods/` containing `RememberCardSort.dll`, `RememberCardSort.json`, and `RememberCardSort.pck`.

The remembered sort is stored in `RememberCardSort.cfg` next to your STS2 save data:

- **Windows:** `%APPDATA%\Slay the Spire 2\mod_configs\`
- **Linux:** `~/.local/share/Slay the Spire 2/mod_configs/`

## Uninstalling

Delete the `RememberCardSort/` folder from your STS2 `mods/` directory. Optionally delete `RememberCardSort.cfg` from `mod_configs/` (paths above) to wipe the remembered sort.
